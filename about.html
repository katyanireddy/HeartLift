About HeartLift

HeartLift is a dedicated platform designed to support young people through the challenging journey of heartbreak recovery. We understand that navigating the emotional turmoil of a breakup can be overwhelming, and our mission is to provide a safe, empowering, and enriching space for healing and growth.

Our Mission

At HeartLift, we believe that every young person deserves access to the resources and support needed to overcome heartbreak and emerge stronger. Our platform offers a comprehensive suite of tools, including personalized daily plans, mood tracking, AI-powered coaching, and a vibrant community forum, all tailored to the unique needs of young adults.

Our Values

Empathy
We are committed to truly understanding each user’s emotional journey and providing genuine, compassionate support every step of the way.
Community
We believe in the power of connection and sharing. HeartLift fosters a safe, inclusive community where users support and uplift each other.
Empowerment
We help users rediscover their inner strength with actionable tools and guidance designed for personal growth and transformation.

Our Approach

HeartLift’s approach is grounded in evidence-based methods and psychological principles. Our daily plans are designed to promote emotional regulation, self-reflection, and positive coping strategies. The AI coach provides personalized guidance and support, adapting to each user’s progress and needs. Our community forum offers a space for peer support and shared healing.

Meet the Team

Our leadership brings experience and passion in youth mental health, psychology, and technology, united by a shared commitment to helping young people. Many of our team members have personal experiences with heartbreak, which fuels our commitment to empathy, empowerment, and connection. Our dedication to providing a safe, effective, and empowering resource for young adults shines through in every aspect of HeartLift.
